Surface Disinfectant

Simply spray (fine mist spray) and leave to dry. Oxybite Ag+ kills most microorganism within 5 seconds of contact. The silver in Oxybite Ag+ will continue to disinfect long after the hydrogen peroxide has evaporated. It is safe to use on your skin, clothes, hair, food, pets, vehicles and most other surface.

Spatial Fogging

Manual methods are the first choice to disinfect surfaces, but for combating airborne pathogens and disinfecting large areas that require rapid re-entry of treated areas, fogging is an ideal solution. Space can be used and entered approximately 45 min (when dry) after fogging.

Hand Sanitizer

Our hand sanitizer contains 70% alcohol as well as moituriser to protect your hands from regular use of sanitizer. Combine Oxybite Ag+ 3% and Oxybite Hand Sanitizer to ensure the protection of the whole family.
